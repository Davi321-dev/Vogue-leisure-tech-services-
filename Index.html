About Us

Vogue Leisure Tech Services is a sole-proprietorship legally operating from Abuja, Nigeria. We specialize in building polished, reliable websites and mobile applications for small businesses, startups, and individual entrepreneurs. Our approach combines modern design, accessible code, and practical product thinking to help clients launch and grow their digital presence.

We are a non-physical (remote) company — which means we deliver projects and support fully online, communicating by email, phone, and virtual meetings. This allows us to serve clients across Nigeria and internationally with flexible schedules and competitive pricing.

Services

  • Website Development — Responsive websites (landing pages, business sites, e-commerce, CMS).
  • Web Applications — Custom SaaS, dashboards, admin panels, integrations.
  • Mobile Apps — Cross-platform mobile apps (React Native / Flutter recommendations).
  • Maintenance & Support — Hosting, updates, security patches, backups.
  • UI/UX & Design — Wireframes, prototypes, and pixel-perfect interfaces.

How We Work

  1. Discover — Project goals, users, scope, and timeline.
  2. Design — Wireframes and visual mockups for approval.
  3. Build — Development with iterative reviews and testing.
  4. Deliver — Launch, training, and handover materials.
  5. Support — Ongoing maintenance plans available.

We use standard tools (Git, GitHub, Figma, Google Meet / Zoom) and prefer transparent communication throughout the project.

Mission & Values
